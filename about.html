About Us

The Arab Innovation Network (AIN) is a non-profit youth organization that aims to empower Arab youth to innovate and contribute to finding solutions to the challenges facing our Arab societies.

Eye on Talents

Eye on Yourself

A self-development program focusing on personal growth and skill enhancement through workshops and mentoring.

Exhibition

Showcasing innovative projects and creative works by talented youth from across the Arab world.

Innovators Hackathon

A competitive event to develop innovative solutions to real-world challenges in a limited time frame.
